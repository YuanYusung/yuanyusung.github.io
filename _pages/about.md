Welcome to Yusong's personal website!

I am a junior seismologist currently working on **dense seismic array signal processing and imaging**. I am passionate about extracting information on source processes and wave-propagation media from seismic recordings. I'm also an amateur music lover, literature enthusiast, and I enjoy swimming, table tennis and hiking.

I am currently a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in **Geophysics** at **China University of Geosciences (Wuhan)**, and I expect to graduate in **June 2026**. During my studies, I have received the National Scholarship and won the First Prize and Best 
Individual Award in the “Innovation Cup” Chinese University Students Geophysics Knowledge Competition, among other honors. I am currently working under the supervision of [Prof. Qiu Hongrui](https://grzy.cug.edu.cn/qiuhongrui/zh_CN/index.htm), on surface-wave denoising and higher-mode surface-wave separation using dense seismic arrays, and their applications to seafloor and fault-zone imaging, based on **distributed acoustic sensing (DAS)**  and **dense nodal seismic array** observations, respectively. In **September 2026**, I plan to begin my Ph.D. studies in Geophysics at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) under the guidance of [Prof. Yao Huajian](http://yaolab.ustc.edu.cn).

**Manuscript in preparation**
